Cologne

Christmas Markets


One of the best things about being in Germany on your year abroad is you get to experience the traditional German Christmas markets!

These open in late November, and sell a variety of German crafts, jewellery, etc., making it a great opportunity to do some Christmas shopping.

There is also some amazing food - be sure to try a (1/2 meter!) Fleischspiess, Flammlachs and of course some Gluhwein.

Karneval


Traditionally, the "fifth season" (carnival season) is declared open at 11 minutes past 11 on the 11th of the 11th month November. The Carnival spirit is then temporarily suspended during the Advent and Christmas period.

For 6 days in February, the streets of Cologne are full of people in fancy dress in a huge city-wide street party. It's a lot of fun and definitely something you should experience if you're living in Cologne/nearby!

The important 2016 dates are:

- Weiberfastnacht, Thurs 4th February

- Rosenmontag, Monday 8th February

- Aschermittwoch, Wednesday 10th February
